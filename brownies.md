# Brownies

Makes 12

## Ingredients

- 140g flour
- 220g butter
- 200g chocolate 
- 250g icing sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp vanilla

## Method

1. Melt butter and chocolate
2. Add sugar
3. Add eggs
4. Add vanilla, salt, and flour
5. Mix thoroughly
6. Pour onto baking tray
7. Bake at 170C for 25 mins
8. Allow to cool
